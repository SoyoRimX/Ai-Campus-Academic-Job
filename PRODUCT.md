# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Three roles, one platform:

- **Teachers** (daily drivers) — track student progress, triage academic warnings, create personalized study plans, review resumes and interview results. They need fast scanning, clear triage signals, and minimal clicks from alert to action.
- **Admins** (periodic oversight) — manage users/roles/menus, curate the AI knowledge base, verify system health. They need straightforward CRUD with confidence that changes take effect correctly.
- **Students** (self-service) — view their own academic profile, resume, study plans, and interview records; run AI-powered tools (resume generation, job matching, mock interviews). They need clarity and encouragement, not data overload.

All roles share a data-isolation model: teachers see only their assigned students, students see only themselves, admins see everything.

## Product Purpose

AI 原生校园学业就业智能服务平台 — an AI-native platform that helps university teachers guide students through academics and into employment. It replaces scattered spreadsheets and manual check-ins with centralized intelligence: automated academic warnings, AI-generated resumes, job matching, and simulated interviews. Success means teachers spend less time on administrative tracking and more time on actual mentoring.

## Brand Personality

**Professional + Approachable + Smart**

- Professional: credible for educators, authoritative without being cold. Data is trustworthy, actions are clear.
- Approachable: warm enough that students feel encouraged, not judged. The interface invites use, not dread.
- Smart: the AI is a tool that augments human judgment — it surfaces insights, suggests actions, but never overpromises or anthropomorphizes itself.

Reference vibe: Notion — clean, minimal, content-first, structured but warm. Professional without the enterprise sterility.

## Anti-references

- **Enterprise gray**: gray tables, gray forms, gray everything. No "corporate software that ships with the database."
- **Gamified/childish**: no cartoon mascots, no point systems, no "fun" for its own sake. This is a serious academic tool used by professionals.
- **Over-designed dashboards**: the big-screen display serves school leaders; it should be clear and impressive, not a Christmas tree of widgets.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over density** — teachers scan fast; surface what matters, defer what doesn't. One primary action per screen section.
2. **Signal before noise** — warnings and statuses use color and hierarchy to guide attention, not decoration. Red means act; gray means reference.
3. **Trust through transparency** — AI outputs (resumes, job matches, interview scores) show their reasoning. No black-box scores.
4. **Familiar patterns, elevated execution** — uses standard admin UI conventions (sidebar, table, form, modal) executed with care. No novelty navigation or experimental layouts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Key commitments:
- Body text contrast ≥ 4.5:1 against background
- All interactive elements keyboard-accessible
- Form errors announced to screen readers
- Reduced-motion alternatives for all animations
- Color never the sole indicator of status (pair with icons, labels, or patterns)
- Chinese-language interface — font stack optimized for CJK readability
